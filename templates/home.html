Helping Families with Technology

A Passion for Technology and Helping Others

Get Started

User Interface

Create a username and password to access a help center. Register a refugee, and add them to our efficient database. As a user you have the access to edit a refugee and send SMS notifications to their smartphones with a click of a button!

Refugee Registration

Register a Refugee by collecting their name, phone number, and help center they will be staying in. This information will add them to our database making it easier for a refugee to be identified in the future.

Refugee Database Access

Shorten the check in time at a ease by searching for a refugee, checking a refugee in or out of a help center, and sending a SMS notification notifying them of the change. A user are also able to edit or delete a refugee's information if needed.

SMS Notifications

Utilizing the Twilio API, this application is allowed to send SMS notifications notifying refugees of their check in status. If a help center has reached it's maximum capacity, a user is allowed to send a capacity update to refugees who are not checked in their assigned help centers. Notifying them that there are available occupancy in other help centers nearby.
